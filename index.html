What is a Bucket List?

A bucket list is a collection of goals, dreams, or experiences a person aims to achieve or complete within their lifetime. These can range from travel destinations and adventurous activities to personal milestones and skill acquisitions. The term "bucket list" often implies a sense of urgency or importance, encouraging individuals to live life to the fullest by actively pursuing their aspirations before they "kick the bucket," or pass away.
